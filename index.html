About Me

Hi, my name is Hank Teicheira and I am starting my journey into the world of web development. Before entering the realm of computer programming, I studied winemaking at UC Davis, where I developed a passion for creating unique products. In my last year in school, I started a small, organic vegetable farm just outside of Davis, California, which grew beautiful produce for sale at Sacramento and Bay Area restaurants.


My love for producing things definitely fueled my interest in web development, and I plan to incorporate my interests in food and farming into developing meaningful applications to benefit producers and consumers in those industries. Outside of computer programming, I love to garden, hike, cook, and forage for wild mushrooms.
